ABOUT US

The RGU Marketing Society was founded in 2020. Our mission is
to be a fully inclusive social and academic society for all
students with an interest in marketing.
We are responsible for organising events that increase knowledge
and awareness of the marketing industry as well as providing key
networking opportunities.
We also have links to Marketing Society Scotland

OUR AIMS

To promote the study and enjoyment of marketing within RGU and in the community.
To facilitate students across all disciplines in the learning of marketing
development by providing information and advice.
To arrange interesting and engaging educational courses, seminars, workshops
and tours, inviting industry speakers and
professionals to participate and share experience and knowledge.
To engage with marketing on campus through a wide range of initiatives lead
by students
To promote networking between the Marketing Society members and industry
to facilitate future employment opportunities.
